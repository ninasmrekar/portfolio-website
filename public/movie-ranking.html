The Best Movies According to Nina

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Pride and Prejudice

This is in my humble opinion the best novel written by Jane Austen and the movie adaptation released in 2005 is my favourite adaption of the novel.

Star Wars

There is no way I am picking only one Star Wars movie (I love all of the first six movies). These movies just have to be watched by anyone who loves sci-fi or fantasy.

Harry Potter

These movies are heartwarming and action-packed at the same time. They always remind me of childhood and of reading the books for the first time.
